A BRIEF HISTORY ABOUT US

Uganda Martyrs SS Namugongo was founded in 1967 by Ms.Hellena Namazzi,Yusuf Mugerwa,Msgr.Lawrence Mbuga and Ssemuly Peter.Our school was at first a single sex school meant to boost the girl child education in Uganda.But this was later changed after the appointment of JC Muyingo the current minister of education as head teacher. The male gender was then introduced into the school setting hence making it the mixed school you see today.With the introduction of Mr.Ssemulya Rex as the new deputy academics, our grades drastically improved hence making us the great institution we are right now.
